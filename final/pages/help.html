FAQ

  • Q: What kind of paper do you print your cards on?
  • A: The decks we sell utilize a variety of stocks and finishes. The most common stock is our trademark thin-stock preferred by cardists, printed by the US Playing Card Co. with an air cushion finish. As well, you can find each exact paper stock and finish on the deck’s description page.
  • Q: What size are your playing cards?
  • A: All of our playing cards are cut to US Standard Poker Playing Card size: 2.5 inches wide by 3.5 inches long.
  • Q: Do you offer discounts for large orders?
  • A: At this time, we do not offer any discounts based on the size of an order. What you see is what you get.
  • Q: Do you ship to my country?
  • A: Purchases of any kind made through our webshop are currently available for shipment to addresses within the United States and Canada.
  • Q: Where is my order?
  • A: You can track your order once it has shipped with the tracking number provided in the shipping notification email that is sent to the email address you provided at checkout.
